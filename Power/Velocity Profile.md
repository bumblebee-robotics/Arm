$\theta_{min} \leq \theta \leq \theta_{max}$

$\theta = 2\theta_{acc}+\theta_{const}$
$2\theta_{acc} = \omega_{max}t_{a}$
$\theta_{const} = w_{max}t_{const}$

Positive $\to$ Const $\to$ Down $\to$ Zero $\to$ Negative $\to$ Const $\to$ Positive

$\theta_{min} \to \theta_{max}$ in $t_{a}$
$\theta_{max} - \theta_{min} = \nabla \theta$
$\nabla \theta = \frac{1}{2}\left( \frac{\omega_{max}}{t_{a}} \right) t^2_{a} = \frac{1}{2}\omega _{max}t_{a}$
Let: $w_{max} = 6 \cdot71 = 426 \frac{deg}{s}$, $\nabla \theta=110 deg$
$110 =\frac{1}{2}426t_{a} \to$ $t_{a} = 0.5s$
